About Koji

Hey there! I'm Koji, a passionate digital artist who loves creating vibrant characters and fan art. My journey started with doodling on napkins and has evolved into a full-blown obsession with pixels, colors, and storytelling through art.

I specialize in character design, particularly original characters (OCs) and fan art from various anime, games, and pop culture. Each piece I create tells a story and brings a little more joy into the world.
